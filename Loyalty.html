Havells FAQ

FAQs

WHAT IS HAVELLS HAPPINESS? +
Havells Happiness is a mobile number-based loyalty program designed exclusively for Havells loyal consumers which offers great savings, priority service and much more.
HOW DO I ENROLL IN THE HAVELLS HAPPINESS PROGRAM? +
You can enroll in the Havells Happiness Program using your registered mobile number through the official website.
HOW DO I REGISTER MY PRODUCT WITH HAVELLS? +
Login to your account and register your product using the serial number mentioned on the product.
IS THERE ANY REGISTRATION FEE? +
No. Registration for the Havells Happiness Program is completely free.
WHAT IS MY MEMBERSHIP NUMBER? +
Your registered mobile number acts as your membership number.
HOW MANY TOKENS DO I EARN? +
The number of reward tokens depends on the product purchased and the current offers.
WHERE CAN I REDEEM MY TOKENS? +
You can redeem your reward tokens from your Havells Happiness account after logging in.
CAN I REDEEM TOKENS IN THE SAME TRANSACTION? +
No. Tokens become available only after successful product registration and verification.
